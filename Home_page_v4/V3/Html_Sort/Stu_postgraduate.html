About postgraduate program

As a comprehensive research university, we offer graduate programs involving a variety of disciplines, including taught masters and research Masters.

We have excellent and dedicated teachers and management team to ensure the efficiency and completeness of the project.

Admission Requiments

Your undergraduate courses and grades are particularly important for applying for a master's program. Please provide your report card. We will comprehensively consider the relevance of your undergraduate major and the project you are applying for. At the same time, a qualified language score (IELTS or TOEFL) is also required.

At this stage, we must also consider the comprehensive ability of the applicant. Including whether they have scientific research achievements, papers, sufficient practical experience, etc.
