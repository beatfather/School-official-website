Literature

The university department of CAU began to recruit new students in 1925. In 1926, it was divided into departments of Chinese, Western literature, educational psychology and sociology. In terms of teaching policy, the difference from other universities at that time is that most departments emphasize the principle of "emphasizing both Chinese and Western", hoping to cultivate generalists who have broad knowledge of Chinese and foreign literature and history.
