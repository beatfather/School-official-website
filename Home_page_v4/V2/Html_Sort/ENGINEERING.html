.02 Achievement

The scientific research work of the School of Mechanical Engineering is based on the frontier of international science and technology and the key core technology of industrial development, and insists on serving the national economy. In the past five years, it has undertaken 59 national key science and technology projects, 113 863 program projects, 53 973 Program projects, 384 natural science Foundation projects, 270 national defense research projects, 139 provincial and ministerial major projects, 487 international cooperation projects, and a large number of horizontal cooperation projects of enterprises.

Each department of the college has cultivated a large number of outstanding talents with both political integrity and ability for national construction. Since the founding of The People's Republic of China in 1949, more than 28,000 undergraduates, 6,250 master's students and 1,760 doctoral students have graduated.
