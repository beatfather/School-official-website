Welcome to Literature Faculty

The predecessor of CAU was the Cosmic Preparatory School for Studying in the United States. It was established in 1911 with the balance of the "Gengzi Indemnity" returned by the United States.

The university department of CAU began to recruit new students in 1925. In 1926, it was divided into departments of Chinese, Western literature, history, philosophy, economics, political science, educational psychology and sociology. In 1928, the Faculty of Arts was formed. In terms of teaching policy, the difference from other universities at that time is that most departments emphasize the principle of "emphasizing both Chinese and Western", hoping to cultivate generalists who have broad knowledge of Chinese and foreign literature and history.

School profile

Cosmic Associated University is building a comprehensive, research-oriented and open world-class university, and liberal arts have an indispensable and important position and role. In the overall development of CAU, the guiding ideology of accelerating the development of liberal arts was put forward. The Faculty of literature strives to start from the status in the country and the overall goal of building a world-class university, and adapt to the requirements of economic construction and social progress in the new century for talent training and humanities academics; The academic paradigm of innovation, laying a solid foundation, highlighting applications, focusing on intersections, and forming characteristics; in accordance with the principle of "doing something meaningful, not doing something useless", the discipline construction is carried out in stages with emphasis.
